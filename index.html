Welcome

The Nepean Community Gardens were created in 1991 and they are one of the largest community gardening sites in Ottawa. We have over 200 plots and around 150 gardeners! We have three types of plots available for rent from May through October:
Raised Beds
4x12'
Annuals
20x30'
Perennials
20x15'
If you would like to rent one of our plots, please create an account and join our waiting list.

Raised beds are often available within a year or even mid-season. Annual plots are rototilled every spring, and they take an average of 2+ years. Perennials plots are entirely maintained by the gardener renting them. They take an average of 3+ years and are only available to existing members.

Preference is given to active NAGA members on all plots.

Explore the garden

We are located at 239 Viewmount Drive in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. When visiting, please be respectful of our gardners by not touching any of the plants and picking up after your pets.
