Welcome!

The Nepean Community Gardens were created in 1991 and they are one of the largest community gardening sites in Ottawa. We have over 200 plots and around 150 gardeners. The plots are available for rent from May through October.

We have three types of plots available:
  • Raised beds are 4 by 12 ft.
  • Annual plots are 20 ft by 30 ft, and are rototilled each spring.
  • Perennial plots are 20 by 15 ft and are entirely maintained by the gardener renting them. You can plant annuals in these plots as well!
If you'd like to rent one of our plots, you can create an account and join our waiting list. Raised beds are often available within a year or even mid-season. Annuals take an average of 2+ years to become available. Perennials are only available to existing members, and they take an average of 3+ years.
