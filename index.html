Welcome

The Nepean Community Gardens were created in 1991 and they are one of the largest community gardening sites in Ottawa. We have over 200 plots and around 150 gardeners! We have three types of plots available for rent from May through October:
Raised beds
4x12'
Annuals
20x30'
Perennials
20x15'
If you would like to rent one of our plots, please create an account and join our waiting list.

Rates
  • Raised bed: $20
  • Half annual plot: $20
  • Full annual plot: $40
  • Oversized annual plot: $50
  • Perennial plot: $20
  • Oversized perennial plot: $30
  • Locker: $5
Annual plots are rototilled every spring. Perennials are only available to active NAGA members and they are entirely maintained by the gardener renting them. Rates are per plot per year. Preference is given to active NAGA members on all plots.

Explore the garden

We are located at 239 Viewmount Drive in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. When visiting, please be respectful of our gardeners by not touching any of the plants and picking up after your pets.
